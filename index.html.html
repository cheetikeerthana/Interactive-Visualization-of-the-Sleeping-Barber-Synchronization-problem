How the Sleeping Barber Problem Works

The Problem

The barber sleeps when there are no customers. When a customer arrives, they check if the barber is sleeping. If so, they wake the barber and get a haircut. If the barber is busy, they wait in a chair. If no chairs are available, the customer leaves.

The Solution

Using semaphores to synchronize the barber and customer threads ensures that only one customer is served at a time, the barber sleeps when there are no customers, and customers are handled correctly when chairs are available or full.
